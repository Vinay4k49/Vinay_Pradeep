Our Comprehensive Dental Services

At 100SMILES Dental Care, we offer a full spectrum of preventive and restorative care to keep your smile healthy and beautiful. Our experienced dentists are well-equipped to handle common dental issues, offering diagnostics, treatments, and surgeries when necessary.

What We Offer

We are dedicated to providing services that nurture the health and happiness of our clients, including:

  • Routine Wellness Check-ups
  • Teeth Cleanings
  • Dental Implants
  • Orthodontics
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Root Canal Treatment
  • Emergency Dental Care
  • Diagnostics and X-Rays
  • Patient Education
  • Nutritional Guidance
  • Oral Hygiene Tips

We prioritize patient education, ensuring you have the knowledge and resources to make informed decisions about your dental health.

Annual Dental Check-Up Package

Our 10% off Annual Dental Check-Up package includes the following services:

  • Physical Examination: A thorough examination of your overall dental health.
  • Cleanings: Ensuring your teeth are clean and free from plaque and tartar.
  • X-Rays: Assessment and preventive measures against common dental issues.
  • Dental Check-Up: Evaluation of your dental health and advice on dental care.
  • Nutritional Guidance: Discussion about your diet and nutritional needs.
  • Blood Tests or Laboratory Work (if necessary): Additional diagnostic tests may be recommended.
